Test Info

WPM Calculation

Words Per Minute (WPM) is calculated by taking the total number of characters typed divided by 5 (standard word length), then divided by the time in minutes.

Accuracy

Accuracy is the percentage of correct characters out of the total characters typed.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Tab Restart Test
  • Esc Pause / Resume
  • Ctrl + Shift + P Toggle Theme

Typing Tips

  • Keep your wrists slightly elevated.
  • Focus on accuracy first; speed will follow.
  • Don't look at the keyboard!

About TuneHub

TuneHub is a minimalistic typing test website. It features many test modes, an account system to save your typing speed history, and user-configurable features such as themes, sounds, and more.

It is designed to be simple, clean, and distraction-free. The site is open source and can be found on GitHub.
